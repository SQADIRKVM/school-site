Facilitated Campus

DUHSS Panakkad is an institution with a long and impressive legacy of first class educational quality and performance.

Darul Uloom Higher Secondary School, located in Panakkad, Malappuram, is one of the most renowned schools in the state. With over 1400 students studying in various classes, and teaching staff and non-teaching staff as per need, it aims to provide educational excellence to all, irrespective of their status, religion, caste, color or creed. Education does not come by chance. Rather, it takes fervor and diligence. A great school and faculty are the best things that can happen to an aspiring child. This is where a child begins forming healthy habits.

The school follows the syllabus drafted by the Govt. of Kerala, and it makes every effort to make learning enjoyable at each stage. Classes last six days with Sundays off. In addition to these holiday breaks, other holidays will be observed by the school that are nationally or regionally significant and mandated by the government.

Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ will answer most of your potential queries .However, be happy to speak us, If you can’t realize your answer.

  • Where is this school located ?

    Darul Uloom Higher Secondary School-Panakkad, Malappuram is located near Gmlps Kolmanna of Malappuram, Malappuram in the state of Kerala in India .

  • Does this school offer Hostel facility ?

    Darul Uloom Higher Secondary School-Panakkad, Malappuram does not offer hostel facility. This school does not have any provisions for residential scholars. Darul Uloom Higher Secondary School-Panakkad, Malappuram is a Day School .

  • What facilities are available in this school ?

    Darul Uloom Higher Secondary School-Panakkad, Malappuram offers the following facilities: Electricity powered Classrooms, Library, Playground, Computers .

  • Is this school co-educational ?

    Yes, Darul Uloom Higher Secondary School-Panakkad, Malappuram is co-educational. This school offers education irrespective of the student's gender .

  • Students transit facility available ?

    School provides transport facility for girls in its 10km radius. For boys, the facility is not accessible .
