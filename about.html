Welcome To Darul Uloom Higher Secondary School Panakkad

The school is committed to providing high-quality education and promoting a moral environment.

In addition to its exemplary academic performance and amazing results, DUHSS adds another golden feather to Malappuram's educational cap. As a result of its distinctive features, the innovative methods and the trust it has managed to gain in the hearts of supportive parents and educators, the school stands out amongst the many others in the field. Year after year, the increasing number of parents vying to secure an admission highlights our reputation as one of the most sought after schools in the area.

WHO WE ARE

Darul Uloom Higher Secondary School, located in Panakkad, Malappuram, is one of the most renowned schools in the state. With over 1400 students studying in various classes, and teaching staff and non-teaching staff as per need, it aims to provide educational excellence to all, irrespective of their status, religion, caste, color or creed. Education does not come by chance. Rather, it takes fervor and diligence. A great school and faculty are the best things that can happen to an aspiring child. This is where a child begins forming healthy habits.

With an underlying commitment to quality education, DUHSS Panakkad focuses on preparing students to acquire knowledge through modern methods of learning and face the challenges of the world. Darul Uloom HSS Panakkad, run by the DUHSS School Association, is managed by an innovative group of socially-conscious individuals who are well respected in their fields. Darul Uloom HSS Panakkad, established in 1979, is a private aided English medium school tasked with spreading the value of education among the aspiring students. Our faculty at the school are adept at employing the right teaching methods and helping students in absorbing knowledge while engaging in the right teaching methods. The teachers at the school play a crucial role in shaping students internally as well as externally. As a guide, teachers play a crucial role in molding the lives and futures of students both inside and outside of the school.

The school follows the syllabus drafted by the Govt. of Kerala, and it makes every effort to make learning enjoyable at each stage. Classes last six days with Sundays off. In addition to these holiday breaks, other holidays will be observed by the school that are nationally or regionally significant and mandated by the government.

OVERVIEW

DARUL ULOOM HIGHER SECONDARY SCHOOL-PANAKKAD was established in 1979 and it is managed by the Pvt. Aided. It is located in Urban area. It is located in MALAPPURAM block of MALAPPURAM district of Kerala. The school consists of Grades from 8 to 12. The school is Co-educational and it doesn't have an attached pre-primary section. The school is Not Applicable in nature and is not using school building as a shift-school. Malayalam is the medium of instructions in this school. This school is approachable by all weather road. In this school academic session starts in April. The school has Private building. It has got 5 classrooms for instructional purposes. All the classrooms are in good condition. It has 2 other rooms for non-teaching activities. The school has a separate room for Head master/Teacher. The school has Pucca boundary wall. The school has electric connection. The source of Drinking Water in the school is Tap Water and it is functional. The school has 13 boys toilet and it is functional. and 18 girls toilet and it is functional. The school has a playground. The school has a library and has 2515 books in its library. The school does not have ramp for disabled children to access classrooms. The school has 20 computers for teaching and learning purposes and all are functional. The school is having a computer aided learning lab. The meal is Provided and Prepared in School Premises providing mid-day meal.
Our Mission
